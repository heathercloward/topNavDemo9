Target Data Services Remediation V All Tabs

Use this page to Register Target Data Services Remediation Rules.

To access this page:

  1. Access Transform.
  2. Click the Targets icon on the Process Area Launch page.
  3. Click the Data Services Reports icon for a Target.
  4. Click the Vertical View icon for a report.
  5. Click the Remediation Information tab.
  6. Click the Remediation icon.
