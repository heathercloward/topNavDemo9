Schedule – Configure

Use this page to Create Schedules.

To access this page:

  1. Select Common > Tools > Schedules in Navigation pane.
  2. Click Vertical View.
  3. Click link for Recurrence ID.

The configuration of this page depends on the Recurrence ID:

  • Minutes – Every ## minutes – Example: Every 30 minutes
  • Hourly – Every ## hour(s) – Example: Every 2 hour(s)
  • Daily – Every ## day(s) – Example: Every 2 day(s)
  • Daily – Every weekday
  • Weekly – Recur every ## week(s) on specific weekdays – Example: Recur every 3 week(s) on Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday
  • Monthly – Fixed date – Example: Day 1 of every 3 month(s)
  • Monthly – Variable date – Example: The third weekday of every 2 month(s)
  • Yearly – Fixed date – Example: Every October 19
  • Yearly – Variable date – Example: The third Sunday of March
